## How Can I Contribute?

### Reporting Bugs
* Check the **Issues** tab to see if the bug has already been reported.
* If not, open a new issue. Include your system specs (though we assume you're on Arch!), your ZSH version, and steps to reproduce the error.

### Suggesting Enhancements
We love new ideas for retro aesthetics or media visualizations!
* Open an issue with the tag `enhancement`.
* Describe the feature and why it would be a "vibey" addition to the project.

### Pull Requests
1. **Fork** the repository.
2. **Create a branch** for your feature (`git checkout -b feature/cool-new-feature`).
3. **Commit** your changes with clear messages.
4. **Push** to your branch and open a **Pull Request**.

## Style Guidelines
* Maintain the **minimalist** and **retro** aesthetic.
* Ensure code is compatible with **Qt 6**.
* Do not change color palette and scheme.
* Test your changes specifically within a **ZSH** environment.

## Community & Conduct
By participating in this project, you agree to abide by the same spirit of freedom and respect outlined in our GPL-3.0 License. Be helpful, be direct, and keep it minimalist.
